About

Hi! My name is Philipp Ross and I'm a computational biologist working in the Llinás Lab at the Pennsylvania State University where I am studying transcriptional regulation in the deadliest human infecting parasite that causes Malaria, Plasmodium falciparum.

Research Interests

I'm currently applying to graduate programs that incorporate the following flavors of research:

  • Evolutionary dynamics of genomes, transcriptomes, metabalomes, molecular pathways, and organisms
  • Genome-scale functional analysis of individual DNA, RNA, and protein elements, and the pathways they're involved in
  • Synthetic biology to combine the first two
I'm particularly interested in exploring the molecular diversity of non-model organisms.
